The Kubuntu Focus Control Center empowers you to easily control and monitor your system power, performance, and fans. It is based on the excellent Control Center created by the team at TUXEDO Computers GmbH.

To customize your system, select Profiles at the top left, and then hover over the desired profile to see a battery and AC icon. If you would like to use the profile on battery, click the battery icon. Similarly, if you would like to use the profile on AC, click the AC icon. Then click on the disk icon to enable your choice. There are three preset profiles (Default, Cool and breezy, and Powersave extreme), and you can also create your own.
